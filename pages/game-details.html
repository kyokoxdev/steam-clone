About This Game

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world, action-adventure RPG set in the megalopolis of Night City, where you play as a cyberpunk mercenary wrapped up in a do-or-die fight for survival. Improved and featuring all-new free additional content, customize your character and playstyle as you take on jobs, build a reputation, and unlock upgrades. The relationships you forge and the choices you make will shape the story and the world around you. Legends are made here. What will yours be?

CREATE YOUR OWN CYBERPUNK

Create a character in your own style, complete with a background to flesh them out, and climb the ladder of Night City. Choose your life path to approach different situations differently, acquire cyberware to enhance your abilities, and find new weapons to take out your enemies. The choices you make define who you are in a city that is desperate to turn you into a nobody.

EXPLORE THE CITY OF THE FUTURE

Night City is a neon-lit metropolis full of unforgettable characters, automated vehicles, and peculiar robots. A place where trust is hard to come by and violence lurks around every corner. Discover the city's unique districts, each with their own identity and populace. Immerse yourself in a huge variety of activities, quests, and mini-games that combine to create an adventure like no other.

BUILD YOUR LEGEND

Go on daring adventures and build relationships with unforgettable characters whose fates are shaped by the choices you make. Become a mercenary, cyberpunk samurai, netrunner, techie, or nomad, steal cyber implants, experience "braindances," slice through enemies with your mantis blades, drive recklessly, or hack enemies and technology alike—or combine these approaches to forge your own path.

Key Features

  • Open World

    Explore the vast, detailed city of Night City with its distinct districts and dynamic day/night cycle.

  • Branching Storyline

    Make choices that significantly impact the story with multiple possible endings.

  • Character Customization

    Create and customize your character with extensive options for appearance, skills, and abilities.

  • Dynamic Combat

    Engage in fast-paced combat with a variety of weapons and cyberware abilities.
